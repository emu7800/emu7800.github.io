EMU7800
Classic Atari 2600 and 7800 games

Welcome to the home of the EMU7800 emulator!

EMU7800 emulates the Atari 7800 ProSystem video game console (c. 1987)
that was also backward-compatible with the older and more popular
Atari 2600 Video Computer System.

EMU7800 runs on Windows 10/11 computers.

Download the latest version here from GitHub!

The installer does not require elevation (i.e., no administrator permission required.)

Game state is maintained at %USERPROFILE%\Saved Games\EMU7800\

EMU7800 was originally completed in 2003 as an experiment in .NET programming.
The current version targets .NET8 on Win32.

Support for Stelladaptor and 2600-daptor, 2600-daptor II is included.
Xbox 360 controllers for Windows also supported.

EMU7800
Classic Atari 2600 and 7800 games

Welcome to the new home of the EMU7800 emulator!

EMU7800 emulates the Atari 7800 ProSystem video game console (c. 1987)
that was also backward-compatible with the older and more popular
Atari 2600 Video Computer System.

EMU7800 runs on Windows 10 computers.

Choose your preferred download package below.
The installers do not require elevation (i.e., administrator permission.)
Current version is v5.0.0:

.msi installer
.exe self-extracting installer
.zip bundle

Game state is maintained at %USERPROFILE%\Saved Games\EMU7800
For the adventurous, the source code bundle is available here.

EMU7800 was originally completed in 2003 as an experiment in .NET programming.
It has been ported to the subsequent litany of UWP and Silverlight platforms
that no longer seem to have much of a future.
The current incarnation targets the .NET5 platform on Win32.

Support for Stelladaptor and 2600-daptor, 2600-daptor II is included.
Xbox 360 controllers for Windows also supported.
